## Context a cold agent needs (do not re-derive)

- **Verbatim:** commingled labelers **represent what you believe is the best labeling for something —
  so mixing them diversifies your DNA.** Turning the gears cascades forward **things you have heard —
  not as things you would endorse, but for others to see** whether they prefer a stranger's label.
  Because **labels are signed by their labelers**, a divergent label carries *who and what* made it:
  learn from it, decide which you like better, and **if you like theirs, adopt it** — signature
  intact. Attributed cultural diffusion: pass along without endorsing; the next person chooses on the
  merits, not the source's authority.
- **What exists:** §R's signature discipline + the cracked-agent detector; `gesture.mjs gatedAttest`
  (the signed-label attestation); jury-by-gesture (§Q/#80); `transfer.mjs` verify-from-anyone /
  act-on-friend-list; consent-and-nonce (pseudonymous, revocable).

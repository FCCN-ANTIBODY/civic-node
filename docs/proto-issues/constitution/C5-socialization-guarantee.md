## Context a cold agent needs (do not re-derive)

- **Verbatim:** we are **free agents moving around who can rush together — and we already do.** The
  mission: a socialization framework guaranteeing that people you encounter, *if they also had this
  tool*, could take it and go almost a **random direction** with it — hand it to **all twenty-eight
  people you came across** (more than you'll ever scan QR with) and it proliferates on its own. Build
  **strong socialization** AND **your own constitution to shape what you save** in one move.
- **What exists:** §R (promiscuous exchange at contact); #72 (sneakernet); `carrier.mjs` (QR/QR-video
  transport); VISION's exit system + "neighbors, not a graph"; the module-install-from-QR op (#74) is
  how the tool itself propagates.

## Do

scan, §R). The instrument stays singular/pinned (§O) even as it proliferates. No central list.
